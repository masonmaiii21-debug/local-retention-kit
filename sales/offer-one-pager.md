# Local Retention Kit - Client Offer

## Positioning

I help pet grooming shops get more repeat bookings and respond to reviews faster with a lightweight follow-up and review-response kit.

This is not a complicated CRM or subscription software. It is a done-with-you setup that gives the shop reusable messages, a simple tracking sheet, and a daily workflow the owner can actually keep using.

## Best First Customer

Pet grooming shops that match at least two of these:

- 30+ Google reviews
- Owner replies to only some reviews
- Active Instagram or Facebook page
- Offers recurring grooming or bath appointments
- Has visible phone/email/DM contact path
- Small independent shop, not a large franchise

## Core Offer

For **$199**, I will build a 7-day customer follow-up and review-response kit for your shop.

You get:

- 20 review replies in your shop's tone
- 20 rebooking reminders for past customers
- 20 follow-up messages for people who asked but did not book
- A simple tracking sheet
- A 30-minute setup walkthrough
- One 7-day follow-up review
