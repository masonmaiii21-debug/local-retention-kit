# Client Handoff Checklist

Use this checklist after a merchant pays for the Starter, Setup, or Done-for-you package.

## 1. Demo the live page

Open the live site and walk the merchant through the clickable flow.

- Click `生成文案` or `试用 MVP` to jump to the generator.
- Click `看报价` to explain the $99, $199, and $299 package options.
- Click `看 15 天计划` to show how quickly this can be launched.
- Click a vertical niche so the merchant sees the promise and channels change.
- Click sample leads in `今天该跟进谁` to show that customer data can drive messages.

## 2. Set merchant basics

In the sidebar:

- Set `店名` to the merchant name.
- Set `复购周期` based on the business type.
- Pick the closest vertical: pet grooming, beauty care, or repair contractor.

Recommended starting cycles:

- Pet grooming: 35 to 45 days.
- Med spa or beauty: 28 to 60 days.
- Repair contractor: 90 to 180 days, unless the merchant has maintenance plans.

## 3. Import order data

Ask the merchant for a CSV export from their order, booking, POS, or invoice tool.

Accepted columns can include:

- Customer name: `name`, `customer`, `client`
- Service: `service`, `item`, `product`
- Date: `date`, `order_date`, `created_at`
- Value: `amount`, `total`, `price`
- Email: `email`
- Phone: `phone`

After import, the app merges repeat customers and marks leads that need follow-up or rebooking.

## 4. Generate the first follow-up batch

For each priority customer:

1. Click the customer in the follow-up list.
2. Confirm the service and days since contact.
3. Pick the tone.
4. Copy the generated message.
5. Paste it into the merchant's email, SMS, Instagram DM, or CRM.

For the first paid delivery, prepare 20 to 50 real messages. This gives the merchant a concrete result, not just a tool.

## 5. Delivery package

Send the merchant:

- The live page link.
- The exported customer CSV after cleanup.
- A short note explaining which customers should be contacted first.
- The first batch of generated messages.
- A 7-day check-in date.

## 6. Upsell path

After 7 days, ask:

- Did any customer reply?
- Which message sounded most natural?
- Which service should we push next?
- Do you want this updated weekly?

Offer a recurring service if they want weekly customer follow-up, review replies, or campaign copy.
