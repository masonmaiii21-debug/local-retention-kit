# Ready-to-Send Sample Packs

Use these when a prospect replies with interest, asks for examples, or says "send it over." These are free samples only. Do not include pricing until after the prospect reacts positively to the samples.

Here are 3 quick examples in Austin Pet Stylist's tone:

1. Review reply

Hi, Austin Pet Stylist here. If your pup is ready for a maintenance bath between haircuts, I can send a couple of easy appointment options for this week or next.

3. Inquiry follow-up
Hi, just checking in from Austin Pet Stylist. If you still need help choosing the right grooming option, reply here and I can make the next step simple.
